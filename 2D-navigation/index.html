The National Scholarships Portal

The National Scholarships Portal (NSP) is a digital platform introduced by the Government of India to streamline the scholarship application and disbursement process for various centrally sponsored scholarships.

Key features and benefits of NSP

  • Centralized Application Process
  • Multiple Scholarship Schemes
  • Transparency and Accountability
  • Timely Disbursement
  • User-Friendly Interface
  • Mobile Application
  • Dashboards for Authorities
  • Digitization of Documents

Key challenges or Problem areas / Pain points

  • Manual war deployments are often slow and infrequent due to time-consuming manual testing and deployment processes.
  • In larger development teams, integrating code changes from different developers can be challenging, leading to integration conflicts.
  • In case of issues with a release, rollbacks can be challenging and time-consuming.
  • Developers may face issues when deploying code to different environments due to inconsistencies in configuration.
  • Monitoring required to identify the problem or service status and resolve it quickly
